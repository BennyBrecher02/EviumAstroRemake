# Evium Products & Services

## The 5 Service Pillars
1. **Hardware** — L2 and L3 charger selection and supply
2. **Software** — OCPP-based management platform with owner portal + driver app
3. **Installation** — Full-service expert installation including permits and electrical
4. **Rebate Processing** — Federal/state incentive applications and management
5. **Maintenance & Management** — 24/7 monitoring, remote diagnostics, ongoing optimization

## Hardware Lineup

### Level 2 (AC) Chargers
- **EVOCHARGE 32A** — Wall-mounted Level 2, Evium-branded
- **EVOCHARGE 40A** — Higher-power wall-mounted Level 2
- **MaxiCharger AC Elite Business** — Pedestal/commercial L2 unit
- Multiple styles and finishes available; wall-mounted and pedestal options
- Green accent branding consistent with Evium identity

### Level 3 (DC Fast) Chargers
- **Maxi DC Fast 60–240kW** — Mid-range DC fast charger
- **Tellus DC Fast 180kW** — High-power DCFC (dual port, CCS1 + CCS1)
- **Tellus DC Fast 300kW** — Ultra-fast DCFC
- **eMobility (Siemens)** charger units

## Software Platform

### Owner Portal (Web-Based)
- Charging activity insights with detailed reports and statistical charts
- Driver group management with custom access rules, pricing, settings
- Remote start/stop via OCPP 1.6+
- Charger configuration and plug settings
- Real-time status and mapping
- Revenue tracking and financial reporting
- Load management across multiple stations
- Firmware management and remote diagnostics
- 24/7 admin support

### Driver Mobile App (iOS/Android)
- Find nearby chargers + map navigation
- Reserve charging sessions
- Remote start/stop
- Real-time activity tracking + notifications
- QR code scanning + RFID card support
- Trip planner
- Favorites list
- Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal, Authorize.net, Google/Apple Pay)
- 24/7 driver support

## Technical Standards & Certifications
- OCPP 1.6+ compliant (industry standard protocol)
- Open ADR 2.0b compliant (grid demand response)
- PCI DSS certified payment processing
- FIPS 140.2 data security at rest
- Multiple payment gateways supported
- AC & DC charger monitoring
- Multilingual support
