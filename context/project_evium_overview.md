# Evium Charging — Company Overview

**Website:** https://eviumcharging.com  
**Founder:** David Fenig | david@eviumcharging.com | (323) 702-2400  

**What They Do:**  
Turnkey EV charging infrastructure company — installs, operates, and maintains electric vehicle charging stations for property owners across diverse property types. Handles the entire process: research, permits, installation, software, rebates, maintenance, and ongoing management.

**Mission / Tagline:**  
"Electrifying the future. One property at a time."

**Core Value Proposition:**  
Eliminates every barrier to property owners adopting EV charging — capital, expertise, complexity, permits, maintenance — offering a true zero-effort solution. Property owners gain a revenue stream and competitive amenity with zero upfront investment (under their primary "no-cost" model).

**Key Stats & Milestones:**
- 500+ chargers nationwide installed
- Thousands of installations expected in 2024
- Major BPU NJ Grant award
- $6MM+ CEC (California Energy Commission) REACH 2.0 Grant Award
- NEVI (National EV Infrastructure) site award — New Mexico
- Hundreds of chargers with RJ Block Properties portfolio across NJ & NY

**Geographic Footprint:**
- Northeast: Maine (Kittery, Atlantic Pointe), New Jersey (New Brunswick), New York City (Coney Island)
- Southwest: New Mexico (NEVI site)
- West: California (CEC grant project)
- National presence, rapid expansion ongoing

**Key Partners:**
- Siemens eMobility
- TPG
- EV Gateway (NM)
- GSA-approved manufacturers

**Role in Industry:**  
Operates as CPO (Charge Point Operator) for federal programs (NEVI) and state energy commission projects.
