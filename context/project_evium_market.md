# Evium Target Market

## Primary Customer Segments

1. **Multifamily Residential** (Apartments, Condos) — HIGHEST PRIORITY
   - Tenants now expect EV charging as standard amenity
   - Drives tenant acquisition and retention
   - Revenue share with property owner, zero capex

2. **Commercial / Office Spaces**
   - Employee benefit and corporate sustainability positioning
   - ESG/sustainability optics for corporate tenants

3. **Retail & Strip Centers**
   - Creates "charging destination" that drives foot traffic
   - Passive income during customer dwell time

4. **Parking Lots (Public & Private)**
   - Expanding infrastructure in underserved locations
   - High-volume charging opportunity

5. **Medical Facilities**
   - Nursing homes, clinics, hospitals
   - Serving essential infrastructure needs

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Property owners / managers with multiple locations (portfolio approach = scalable)
- Forward-thinking operators seeking future-proof assets
- Organizations with sustainability/ESG mandates
- Those who want revenue streams with zero capital outlay
- Operators in competitive markets needing differentiation

## Customer Pain Points Solved
- Range anxiety across the ecosystem (needs more chargers everywhere)
- No capital/expertise to build charging infrastructure
- Complexity: permits, rebates, installation, maintenance
- Need to stay competitive with modern amenities
- Management burden of post-installation operations
