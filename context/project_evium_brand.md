# Evium Brand Identity

## Visual Identity

### Colors
- **Primary Green:** ~#2DC856 / #3DD55C — bright, energetic lime-green. Used for "EV" in logo, accents on chargers and stickers, CTA elements
- **Dark Teal/Navy:** ~#1A2F3A / #1E3A45 — deep, sophisticated dark teal. Used for "IUM" in logo, backgrounds, professional tone
- **White:** Clean contrast text and backgrounds

### Logo
- "EV" in bright green + "IUM" in white (or dark) — visually splits "EV" out to highlight the electric vehicle focus
- "CHARGING" in smaller green all-caps text below
- Bold, geometric sans-serif letterforms
- High contrast, modern, minimal
- Light version (white/green on dark): `ExistingEviumContextMedia/logo-light.webp`

### Typography
- **Primary Font: Maven Pro** (geometric sans-serif, very clean and modern)
  - Weights available: Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold, ExtraBold, Black
  - Font files: `/Users/bennybrecher/Claude/Code/Evium/CopyOfZippedEviumDataFromGoogleToInspect/Branding/Fonts/Copy of Maven Pro/`

### Overall Aesthetic
- Modern, clean, tech-forward, bold
- Green = eco/energy/electric; Dark teal = trust/stability/professional
- Minimalist with generous whitespace
- Chargers feature green accent rings/branding on black/neutral hardware

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Character
Forward-thinking, mission-driven, confident, empowering, action-oriented, results-focused

**Future-Building:**
- "We're not following the future. We're building it."
- "Smart properties aren't waiting for the future. They're building it."
- "Future-proof your property"
- "Stay ahead of the curve"

**Empowering Property Owners:**
- "Empowering landlords to effortlessly introduce EV charging"
- "Turn unused space into real value"
- "Transform your property into a high-demand destination"
- "Unlock new value for your property"
- "Make the switch before your competitors do"

**Real Results Focused:**
- "Real results. Real returns."
- "ROI that exceeded projections"

**Mission-Driven:**
- "It wasn't to chase a trend"

**Competitive Urgency:**
- "If you're not offering it yet, you're already a step behind"

### Copy Style Notes
- Short, punchy sentences. Active voice.
- Mix of bold statements and warm/consultative framing
- Positions EV charging as inevitable — not optional
- Emphasizes ease (for the property owner) and results
- Avoids jargon-heavy technical language in marketing copy
- Founder voice is grateful, personal, mission-focused
